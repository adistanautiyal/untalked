What is sexual education?
by-Adista(current)

Sexual education is a learning process that provides information and guidance about human sexuality, including reproductive anatomy, sexual intercourse, contraception, sexual orientation, sexual health, and consent. Its aim is to empower individuals with knowledge and skills to make informed decisions about their sexual health and relationships.

What is the difference between sex and gender?
by-Sreehitha(current)

Sex refers to the biological characteristics that define males and females, typically including anatomy, chromosomes, and hormones. Gender, on the other hand, refers to the social, cultural, and psychological attributes, behaviors, and roles associated with being male or female. While sex is typically binary (male or female), gender is more fluid and can encompass a range of identities beyond just male and female, including non-binary, transgender, and genderqueer identities.

What do you mean by sexual orientation?
by-Krisharsh(current)

Sexual orientation refers to a person's enduring emotional, romantic, or sexual attraction to individuals of a particular gender or genders. Common sexual orientations include heterosexual (attraction to people of the opposite gender), homosexual (attraction to people of the same gender), and bisexual (attraction to people of more than one gender). Other orientations include pansexual, asexual, and queer, among others. Sexual orientation is a deeply personal aspect of identity and can vary widely among individuals.
